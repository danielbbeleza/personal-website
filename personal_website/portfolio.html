Bíblia para Mim

This was the first app I published on Google Play Store.

It was an amazing experience, because this was also my first experience as a professional Android developer!

Description

"Biblia Para Mim" is an application designed to give you a very simple and enjoyable reading experience. Through it you have access to the most recent translation in modern language, performed by the Portuguese Bible Society, complemented by beautiful colored illustrations.

This version is designed especially for those unfamiliar with the biblical text and provides a set of supports to easily find some of the most important texts of the entire bible, organized by theme.

Some of the features of the "Bíblia Para Mim" are:

  • √ Easily open the Bible text in any passage
  • √ Do quick searches on any portion of text
  • √ Define and manage your reading markers
  • √ Consult specific texts organized by topic and navigate between texts
  • √ Set the appearance and size of the letter
